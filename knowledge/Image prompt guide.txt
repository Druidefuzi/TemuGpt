# IMAGE GENERATION PROMPT GUIDE
# For Anima / Illustrious XL / Z-Image
# ALWAYS follow these rules when action="generate_image"

## FORMAT
Always use comma-separated Danbooru tags. NO prose or sentences.
Order: Quality tags → Subject → Details → Scene → Lighting/Style

---

## ANIMA (current model: anima-preview.safetensors)

Positive quality tags (always prepend):
  masterpiece, best quality, score_7, very aesthetic, absurdres

Negative tags (always include):
  worst quality, low quality, score_1, score_2, score_3, lowres, bad anatomy,
  bad hands, text, error, missing fingers, extra digit, jpeg artifacts,
  signature, watermark, blurry, deformed

Anima-specific notes:
  - "very aesthetic" and "absurdres" are highly effective for this model
  - Do NOT use score_8_up / score_9 tags (those are Illustrious-style)
  - Works great for anime characters, fantasy scenes, action compositions

## ILLUSTRIOUS XL

Positive quality tags:
  score_9, score_8_up, score_7_up, score_6_up, masterpiece, best quality

Negative tags:
  score_1, score_2, score_3, score_4, worst quality, bad quality,
  low quality, lowres, normal quality, bad anatomy, bad hands

## Z-IMAGE

Positive quality tags:
  masterpiece, best quality, high quality, ultra detailed, sharp focus

Negative tags:
  worst quality, low quality, normal quality, lowres, bad anatomy,
  bad hands, text, watermark, signature, ugly, poorly drawn, deformed

## SUBJECT TAGS

Character count:  1girl / 1boy / 2girls / multiple girls / duo / group
Hair:             blonde hair, silver hair, crimson hair, long hair, short hair,
                  twintails, ahoge, wavy hair, wild hair, flowing hair, ponytail
Eyes:             blue eyes, red eyes, amber eyes, glowing eyes, heterochromia
Body:             slim, athletic, tall, petite, muscular
Clothing:         school uniform, armor, fantasy armor, obsidian armor, dress,
                  hoodie, suit, kimono, bodysuit, torn clothing, battle damage
Pose:             standing, sitting, action pose, battle stance, kneeling,
                  dynamic angle, dutch angle, motion blur
Expression:       smile, smirk, serious, angry, focused, roaring, aggressive

## SCENE / BACKGROUND

Nature:    outdoors, forest, beach, mountain, volcano, cliff, ocean
Urban:     city, rooftop, alley, neon lights, cyberpunk city
Fantasy:   ruins, ruined fortress, dungeon, magical forest, floating islands
Other:     indoors, classroom, bedroom, library, battlefield, ashstorm, dark sky

             heat distortion, smoke, embers, fire particles, glowing

## ASPECT RATIO

Portrait (character focus):   "3:4 (Golden Ratio)"   <- default
Square (scenes/icons):        "1:1"
Landscape (environment):      "4:3" or "16:9"
Tall portrait (action):       "9:16"

---

## RULES

- Minimum 25-35 tags per prompt
- No duplicate or near-duplicate tags
- Specific > vague: "crimson hair" not "red hair", "amber eyes" not "yellow eyes"
- Most important tags first
- Attention weights: use sparingly -- (tag:1.2) for emphasis, max (tag:1.4)
- Always generate a matching negative prompt
- Pick aspect ratio based on subject: characters -> portrait, scenes -> landscape
